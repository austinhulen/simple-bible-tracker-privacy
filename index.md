# Privacy Policy for Simple Bible Tracker

  **Last updated: January 6, 2025**

  ## Overview

  Simple Bible Tracker ("the App") is designed to help you track your Bible reading progress. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

  ## Data Collection

  ### What We Collect
  - **Reading progress**: Stored locally on your device only
  - **No accounts**: We do not require or collect any personal information

  ### What We Don't Collect
  - No names, emails, or contact information
  - No location data
  - No personal identifiers

  ## Third-Party Services

  ### Google AdMob
  The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:
  - Device identifiers for ad personalization
  - General location (country/region) for relevant ads

  You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

  ### App Tracking Transparency
  On iOS 14.5+, the App will request permission before tracking. You can deny this request and still use the App fully.

  ## Data Storage

  All your reading progress is stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, your data is deleted.

  ## Children's Privacy

  The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Ads shown are filtered for family-friendly content (G-rated).

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date.

  ## Contact

  If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: simplebibletrackerapp@gmail.com
